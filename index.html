About this Website

This website is the first website and an academic project crafted by Amerogin Kamid, a junior student at Sultan Kudarat State University - Isulan Campus, currently pursuing his undergraduate degree in Computer Science. It was developed as part of a course instructed by Professor Elbren O. Antonio, DIT, who ignited Amerogin's passion for creative technology. Amerogin expresses sincere gratitude to Professor Antonio for this inspiration.

The website's creation spanned two weeks, during which Amerogin, despite tight schedules, meticulously designed it. The process began with conceptualization and involved researching numerous trending website portfolios from 2023 for inspiration. Although the website is still under construction, Amerogin has incorporated all the essentials. He plans to continue refining this personal portfolio even after completing the course. Dr. Elbren emphasized that the website is a representation of Amerogin in the field, serving as a powerful tool for marketing himself and showcasing the projects and works he undertakes in future employments or project auditions.
